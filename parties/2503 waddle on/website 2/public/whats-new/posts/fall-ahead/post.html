Fall... Ahead?

'Sup penguins?

Hope you've been enjoying the Summer Sunset! With tons of items to collect, snowman puffles to adopt, and chill vibes despite all the heat, we hope everyone found something to enjoy about this party. It's wrapping up pretty soon so make sure get that last surf in before the high tide rolls back for fall.

Speaking of fall, there's a lot to look forward to in it! After the party, you can expect the completion of our brand new Boiler Room, a new issue of the Club Penguin Times and the first batch of pages for our long-awaited new permanent catalog hosted in the Puffle Berry Mall, which we've named Puffle Berry's Best! You can find tons of classic outfits and an old friend waiting for you to uncover all it's secrets.

Cover not final

Of course, outside of that, with any new month comes a new party, however, this one's going to be a bit more hush hush until closer to release, it's a big surprise and one we can't wait to share. If waiting isn't your thing though, you might can get an idea of who might be involved if you take a look over in the Beacon Telescope. ;)

We hope you stick around for the future and we hope you've enjoyed everything we've put out so far. You've all made this year wonderful and we're happy to of shared this time with you. See you on the island, and stay cool. Waddle on!
