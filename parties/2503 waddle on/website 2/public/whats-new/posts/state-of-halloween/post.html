UPDATE

State of Halloween

Hello all,

It pains me greatly to say this yet again, but unfortunately the keys to the Mansion are still lost. If you'll allow me a moment to whine, I can explain what exactly led to this point.

I have not had the best month.

I believe it mostly comes down to seasonal depression, but motivation has been at an all-time low for me, as well as the amount of real-life stuff that leaves me unable to get things done here being unusually high this month as well. Those two things combined meant I did a lot of procrastinating. And that is not something I can afford to let happen, given there is nobody else to take up my workload. That's how I end up writing a blog post like this at 1 in the morning.

To be absolutely transparent, because of this, 90% of what came out on October 26th (or the 27th) was made day-of, excluding the minigame reskins, as those were one of the few things that were not my responsibility.

I attempt to present myself as if I have things worked out and am not working until the last second every single time, but in reality that's often what happens with everything that I release. Generally, this isn't a big deal, but it has now gotten to the point of a 48 hour delay, which has never happened before, at least as blatantly as this, and as much as I like to joke otherwise, I hate lying to the fans.

Due to multiple unforeseen technical issues, things outside of my control, as well as hours seeming to just evaporate, I have not yet completed the programming required for all the features of the Mansion. I'm still trying my best to get everything finished, but I still feel as if I've failed for making you all wait so long regardless.

This is why I want to at the very least take a few minutes to keep you all up-to-date, and make sure you're all aware that it was my poor planning and deciding to have a life and mine alone that's caused this.

The party will be extended by a few days once the Mansion does actually release, and I hope that day is tomorrow.

Deeply sorry, and thank you all for your patience,
