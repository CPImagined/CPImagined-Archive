Y'know, a question we've often get asked a lot is... "What's next?" and as much as we'd like to answer that, I first want to go over the question of "What's happened?"
There's been a lot of cool new changes happening to the game these past weeks. We finished up our Superhero Showdown Party (Thank goodness!) and Protobot decided enough was enough and started throwing meteors at us. Too bad we haven't really had a chance to panic yet because we've been so busy looking at our snazzy new map!

Hopefully this helps players old and new have something a little nicer to look at, as well as helping find some of our exclusive features a bit easier, from Blue Puffles being able to join you in Hydro Hopper, to the ever popular Puffle Berry's Best catalog in the Mall.
