How to play on Mac

Hello!

If you're reading this, unfortunately we're having issues with our new Mac client at this time. The old version is available until we can get this fixed.


Step 1

Download the old client here.


Step 2

Download The Unarchiver from the App Store. (It's free!)


Step 3

Right click on clientmacportable.zip and click the "Open with The Unarchiver" option.


Step 4

Open the CPImagined Desktop app inside the folder that was just created.


Step 5

You're done!

If you encounter any issues, please join our Discord and send a message in the #support channel for help!
