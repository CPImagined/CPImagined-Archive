ROCKHOPPER: WHO IS HE?
Rumour Reporter
February 14, 2022

Rockhopper: who is he? Well, he is Club Penguin Imagined's resident pirate, and usually comes around every 2-4 months. He also brings special rare items from other islands he finds in his travels around the seven seas!

We managed to catch up to him before he left after the Holiday Party back in 2021 for an exclusive interview:

Rockhopper: Ahoy! How be ye, matey?

Rumour Reporter: I'm doing great! Could I get an interview?

Rockhopper: Of course matey! What do ye want to know?

Rumour Reporter: First, where do you go when you leave Club Penguin Imagined?

Rockhopper: Well that be an easy one, matey! I always go to Rockhopper Island to stock up on me' supplies and things, then I head off into new adventures!

Rumour Reporter: What do you mean, "new adventures"?

Rockhopper: I go where no pirate has gone before!

Rumour Reporter: Oh, really? I would've thought after nearly 17 years would would've explored the entire world by now!

Rockhopper: Ye would think that wouldn't ye... 'twas hard to leave Club Penguin, then Club Penguin Island after that... I be happy that the penguins still be partyin' o'er there... even though all o' us "mascots" had to leave...

Rumor Reporter: Uh, Rockhopper? Are you ok?

Rockhopper: What? Oh, yeah matey, of course I be alright! Just got lost in some memories...

Rumor Reporter: Ah, anyway, do you think you could tell us one of your famous stories?

Rockhopper: Of course matey! Let's do one o' me favourites!
There be a memory dear to me heart,
It be the time I meet me best friend Yarr!
I was sailin' me ship the Migrator for new lands.
I be havin' all kinds of adventures.
Avast! In the sea there be a strange sight.
It be a piece of driftwood with a puffle atop it!
Of course, they weren't called puffles yet, the penguins over in Club Penguin hadn't even found one yet!
I hoisted the sails and sped to save that puffle's life!
Ay, he warmed to me like a clam to a shell
We be best buds and now he be me first mate!

Rumor Reporter: Bravo! I always love reading about that in your journal in the Book Room.

Rockhopper: Well matey, I think it be time for me to go! I be needin' to pack up the holiday decorations on the Migrator and get 'em stowed away, fair winds!

Rumor Reporter: Thanks for the interview Rockhopper, I'll see you next time!

What an interesting interview! That's all for today, goodbye!
- Rumour Reporter
