THE FAIR IS COMING!
Rumour Reporter
March 1, 2022

This just in: Rookie has ordered in supplies, and seems to be reopening the Amazement Park from 2015!

I made my way over to Rookie through the precarious piles of boxes where he was discussing a Fair attraction with the construction team, headed up by Rory.

"It's gotta be like BOOSH!" he said, "And then over here it'll be like WEEEEEE BAM!"

I can't deny that Rookie has a lot of enthusiasm for the Fair, but I had some concerns about the scale of the project.

Rookie told me, "Don't worry about the scales. The mermaid props are ready to go! The tricky part is all the water."

Well, we've all got our eyes on the Fair, waiting to see what happens. Especially the cleanup crews - they're on high alert!

Rookie has asked me to remind everyone that the Fair is a "No Clown Zone," so don't be getting any clown costumes!

- Rumour Reporter
